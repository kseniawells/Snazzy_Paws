Welcome to Snazzy Paws

At Snazzy Paws Grooming, we believe every dog deserves to look and feel their best. Based in Hamilton, our salon provides a calm and welcoming space where pets are treated with care, patience, and respect. From a simple bath to a full grooming package, our experienced team will ensure that each service is tailored to the needs of your dog.

We take pride in combining professional skills with a friendly approach. Whether it’s a regular tidy-up or a special spa treatment, our goal is to make every visit a positive experience for dogs and their owners.
